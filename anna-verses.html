Anna’s Favorite Bible Verses

These verses help me feel brave when I’m nervous, unsure, or afraid. I hope they help you too! – Anna the Bear

Deuteronomy 31:6

“Be strong and courageous... the Lord your God goes with you.”

Anna says: This is my go-to verse when I feel afraid. God never leaves!

Joshua 1:9

“Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go.”

Try it: Say this verse out loud before starting something new.

Isaiah 41:10

“So do not fear, for I am with you... I will strengthen you and help you.”

Anna says: You don’t have to feel strong to be strong — God gives you His strength.

Psalm 56:3

“When I am afraid, I put my trust in You.”

Try it: Write this verse on a sticky note and put it on your mirror!

2 Timothy 1:7

“For God gave us a spirit not of fear but of power and love and self-control.”

Anna says: God made you bold, kind, and strong — don’t forget it!
