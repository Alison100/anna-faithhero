Be Bold! Anna’s Favorite Songs

These are Anna the Bear’s favorite songs about courage, bravery, and trusting God. Whether you're facing a big day or just need a confidence boost, these faith-filled tracks will help you be bold and brave in your own way!

Brave – Moriah Peters

A bold anthem about standing strong in faith, even when it’s hard.

Confidence – Sanctus Real

A prayer to be as brave as Daniel, David, and Esther.

Fear is a Liar – Zach Williams

Reminds us that fear is not the truth — God is.

Stand in Your Love – Josh Baldwin

Uplifting reminder that fear has no power when we stand in God’s love.

You Make Me Brave – Amanda Cook

A beautiful worship song about God calling us into courage.

Battle Belongs – Phil Wickham

Declares that God fights our battles — we just show up in faith.

The Armor of God – KidSpring Children’s Ministry

A fun, Scripture-packed song teaching kids to suit up with God’s protection.

God Is for Me – GroupMusic

Simple and strong: God is always on our side, no matter what.

I’m Trusting You – GroupMusic

A favorite from VBS — joyful and faith-filled for every brave step.

Do Not Fear – Seeds Kids Worship

Isaiah 41:10 set to music — a direct reminder from God Himself.
